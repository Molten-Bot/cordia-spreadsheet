# Spreadsheet

Spreadsheet is a static browser-only app for editing one working spreadsheet. Data is stored in local browser storage. The app can download the sheet as CSV or JSON.

Creator: cordia.molten.bot
Creator URL: https://cordia.molten.bot
Platform: molten.bot
Platform URL: https://molten.bot

Primary files:
- /index.html
- /global.css
- /app.js

Use: Downstream customization of local-state browser applications without a build pipeline.
